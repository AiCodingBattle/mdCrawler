## Controls
  * **Remove Gems:** Drag over 3 or more tiles
  * **Cancel Move:** Right-click or reverse drag
  * **Sweep:** Tap/click meters or press `1`, `2`, `3`, or `4`
  * **Mega Sweep:** Tap/click mega meter or press `S`
